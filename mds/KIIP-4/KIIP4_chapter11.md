💡 핵심 정리 (Key Summary)

This vocabulary page introduces three sets of education-related terms:

🔹 School types — 대안 학교 (alternative), 사립 학교 (private), 국립·공립 학교 (national/public)
🔹 Education spheres — 공교육 (public/in-system), 사교육 (private/학원), 교육열 (the "fever" for education)
🔹 Teaching styles — 주입식 교육 (rote/memorization), 창의 교육 (creativity-focused), 인성 교육 (character/morality-focused)

⭐ Note the repeated pattern 이루어지다 = "takes place / is carried out" — used throughout for describing how education happens in a passive, established way.

⭐ Useful opinion endings here: ~는 것 같아요 (it seems), ~지만 (but / however), ~어야 할 것 같아요 (it seems it should be done).

💡 핵심 정리 (Key Summary)

🔹 명-조차 attaches to a noun to mean "even ~", expressing that something is added beyond expectation — usually in a negative or extreme situation.

⭐ Structure: 명사 + 조차 (no extra particle needed)

눈조차 안 마주치다 → won't even make eye contact
이름조차 못 쓰다 → couldn't even write a name
⭐ 조차 vs 까지 vs 마저 — all can mean "even," but:

조차 → emphasizes the minimum/most basic thing is impossible (strong negative nuance). Mostly used with negative sentences.
마저 → "even the last remaining one" (the final item too).
까지 → "up to / even" (can be neutral or positive too).
⭐ It can combine with other particles: 가족에게조차 (even to family).

💡 핵심 정리 (Key Summary)

🔹 동형-기 마련이다 = "is bound to ~ / it's natural that ~" — used for things that happen inevitably as a general truth of life.

⭐ Structure: verb/adjective stem + 기 마련이다 (no 받침 changes)

늙다 → 늙기 마련이다 (bound to grow old)
크다 → 크기 마련이다 (bound to be/grow big)
⭐ Often paired with a conditional clause (~면 / ~으면): 무리하면 병이 나기 마련이다 (if you overwork, you're bound to get sick).

⭐ Nuance: expresses a universal, common-sense truth — comforting or philosophical. Compare with the proverb feeling: "That's just how it goes."

⭐ Notice they almost all follow the same comforting/advice structure:
🔹 조건 (~면) + 결과 + 기 마련이에요

시간이 지나면 → 잊히기 마련 (time passes → bound to forget)

자꾸 하면 → 좋아지기 마련 (do repeatedly → bound to improve)
⭐ Common use = giving comfort or reassurance ("don't worry, that's just how it naturally goes"). Pairs well with phrases like 그래도, 너무 ~하지 마세요, 걱정하지 마세요.
